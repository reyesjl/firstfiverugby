The Cardiff Blues

Welsh rugby. Showcasing skill, determination, and the spirit of the game.

The Cardiff Blues Rugby team, based in Cardiff, Wales, competes in the United Rugby Championship.

Established in 1876, they've become renowned for producing talented players who often represent Wales and contribute to British and Irish Lions tours.
